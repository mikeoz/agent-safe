In 3 minutes:
- Bob writes a permission slip
- His AI gets exactly what the slip says — Vital Signs only, read only, 24 hours
- Bob closes the door — one tap, immediate
- The Verification Endpoint confirms: locked out, right now

Every step logged. Every step reversible. That is Agent Safe.
